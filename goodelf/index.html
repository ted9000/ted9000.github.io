Our traditional family run deli has been a mainstay of the local community for decades. As well as our ever-popular range of ready meals, produced on site, we stock pies and savouries from long standing, high quality suppliers. We have excellent cheeses, a wide range of preserves and chutneys, biscuits, cakes and a very competitively priced wine selection.

Our ready meals are priced at, or around, £3.95 for a generous portion and can be bought individually from our shop. Dishes of our produce for family dining and entertaining are available to order. Dishes to order typically serve 8 and we can supply for small or large events. The oven dishes are returnable, and we will advise you precisely on cooking times and oven requirements. We can also supply rice cookers and catering size pans if required.
